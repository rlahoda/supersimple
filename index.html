What makes up a spring classic?

  • One day race
  • Long history
    • Milan-San Remo started in 1907
    • Paris-Roubaix started in 1896
  • Early in the racing calendar
  • Usually have a distinctive characteristic
    • Paris-Roubaix has the cobbles
    • La Fleche Wallonne has the Mur de Huy
    • Gent - Wevelgem has the Kemmelberg
